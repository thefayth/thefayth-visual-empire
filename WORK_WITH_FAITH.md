## Best-Fit Engagements

### AI Website Visual Cleanup

For sites with weak visuals, repeated assets, broken images, generic AI art, inconsistent crops, line-drawn placeholders, or public pages that do not yet carry enough trust.

Outcome: a sharper image system, route-by-route recommendations, replacement plan, and publish-ready handoff.

### AI Brand System Audit

For teams whose public copy, visuals, docs, GitHub surface, and product story do not yet match the strength of the underlying work.

Outcome: a practical brand-fit review with visual rules, rejected patterns, accessibility notes, and next-batch creative direction.

### GitHub / Public Surface Packaging

For private tools that need a credible public GitHub presence without leaking the private engine.

Outcome: public-safe README, docs, status, roadmap, ownership, security, and commercial CTA alignment.

### Local AI Workflow Setup

For builders who want AI help without losing control of local files, receipts, approvals, or production boundaries.

Outcome: workflow map, folder structure, task packet templates, receipt paths, and safety rules.

### Protected File / Provenance Consulting

For people who need better proof, authorship, original-file, and selective disclosure workflows.

Outcome: practical provenance strategy and protected-file workflow design.

## How To Start

Send a short note through [FaithCheltenham.com/contact](https://faithcheltenham.com/contact/) with:

- the public URL, repo, website, or workflow you want reviewed
- the outcome you want the work to make possible
- the timeline or launch window
